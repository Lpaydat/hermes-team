## Key positioning insight

The incumbent pricing band for employer-side assessment is **$79–$599/mo**
(CodeSignal Build→Grow) with enterprise quotes above (HackerRank, Karat). The
candidate-side copilots are free-to-$25/mo. The whitespace for a solo-founder
or indie entrant validated at **$10k MRR** (r/SaaS `1v29zgb`, 2026-07-22) sits
at roughly **$149–$499/mo** for mid-market (50–2,000 employees) buyers who
outgrow a take-home doc but can't afford Karat's managed service.

The defensible wedge is *not* price — it is paradigm: every incumbent is
"AI-resistant" (harder puzzles + proctoring to catch cheaters). The open space
is "AI-native" assessment (evaluate the candidate's ability to steer/audit
AI-generated code), which HN hiring managers are independently converging on
(HN `47415117`, `47722081`). Price competitively with CodeSignal; differentiate
on what is evaluated, not what is charged.
