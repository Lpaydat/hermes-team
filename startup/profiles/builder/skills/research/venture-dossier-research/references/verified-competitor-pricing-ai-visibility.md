### Market segmentation pattern

The field has stratified into clear tiers:
- **Budget / indie** ($20–49/mo): PilotCite Core/Growth, Otterly, RankScale, Atomic AGI
- **Mid-market** ($95–295/mo): Trakkr, Profound Starter, Peec, AthenaHQ, Semrush add-on
- **Enterprise / agency** ($249–499+/mo): Sitefire, Profound Growth, Scrunch, Ahrefs Brand Radar
- **Free / lead-gen**: Lagotto Meter (one-time), AthenaHQ free tier

### Competitive gap pattern (reusable insight)

In nascent markets where 12+ tools exist but the space is <2 years old:
- The field usually stratifies into price tiers quickly (budget / mid / enterprise).
- **Differentiation gaps** typically emerge at the *intersection* of two needs no single tool
  serves well. For AI-visibility: the gap was "AI-readiness" (can AI parse your site?)
  combined with "indie pricing" — Sitefire does readiness but is $249+; PilotCite is cheap
  but only monitors. The gap is the *combination* at a specific price point.
- Look for what the most expensive tool does that the cheapest tools don't, then ask
  whether that feature can be delivered at the lower price point.
