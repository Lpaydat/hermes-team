## Key positioning insight

For an indie-builder distribution tool, the whitespace price band is **$29–79/mo SaaS tier** (above Hypefury/Buffer, below SparkToro Business or an agency). A concierge tier at **$300–500/mo** targets founders who explicitly want to pay someone (validated by HN signal `48787370`).

None of the above tools combine: (1) multi-community thread discovery, (2) authentic reply drafting with human approval, (3) signup attribution back to the sourcing thread. That triple is the net gap.
