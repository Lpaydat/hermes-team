## Key Market Signals (from HN)

1. **Real-time OSINT dashboard** (Shadowbroker) — 312 pts, 2026-03-08. GitHub open source project pulling 15 live global feeds. "I've seen so many of these in the last week alone. I need a realtime OSINT dashboard for OSINT dashboards." — market is forming.

2. **OSINT tools directories are a recurring side project** — 3+ Show HN posts in 2024-2026 (OsintRadar 83pts, R00M 101 53pts, older entries). Comment pattern: "generally useless," "biased towards US," "wrappers/aggregators on other services."

3. **"Slow collapse of critical thinking in OSINT due to AI"** — 446 pts, 2025-04-03. Signals that AI-powered OSINT tools are entering the market but quality/verification is a concern.

4. **Optery (YC W22)** — 223 pts, 2022-03-08. Data removal service (the inverse of OSINT). Validates the personal data ecosystem. "I would definitely pay for the removal of arrest record listings."

5. **Enterprise vs prosumer gap**: All funded platforms gate pricing behind "contact sales." The free/open-source tools are fragmented, US-centric, and criticized as "surface level." This is the whitespace.
